Follow these steps to get free keys. You can use just one, or all of them for maximum reliability.

---

### 1. Abstract API (Recommended - Easiest)
*Limit: 100 free requests/month*

1.  Go to: **https://app.abstractapi.com/users/signup**
2.  Sign up with your email.
3.  Once logged in, verify your email if asked.
4.  On the dashboard, find "Email Validation / Verification".
5.  Click **"Manage"** or **"API Key"**.
6.  Copy the **API Key** (starts with `...`).
7.  Add to `.env.local`: `EMAIL_VERIFY_ABSTRACT_KEY=your_key_here`

---

### 2. ZeroBounce
*Limit: 100 free credits/month (refills monthly)*

1.  Go to: **https://www.zerobounce.net/login/** -> Click "Register".
2.  Sign up and verify your email link.
3.  Log in to the dashboard.
4.  In the menu, go to **"API - Keys & Info"**.
5.  You should see your API key (or click "Create API Key").
6.  Copy the key.
7.  Add to `.env.local`: `EMAIL_VERIFY_ZEROBOUNCE_KEY=your_key_here`

---

### 3. Hunter.io
*Limit: 25 free requests/month*

1.  Go to: **https://hunter.io/users/sign_up**
2.  Sign up using a "Business Email" (Gmail might work but they prefer custom domains).
3.  Go to your profile menu (top right) -> **"API"**.
4.  Copy the **API Key**.
5.  Add to `.env.local`: `EMAIL_VERIFY_HUNTER_KEY=your_key_here`

---

### 4. ApiLayer
*Limit: 100 free requests/month*

1.  Go to: **https://apilayer.com/marketplace/email_verification-api**
2.  Click **"Subscribe for Free"**.
3.  Sign up if you don't have an account.
4.  Once subscribed, your **API Key** will be shown at the top of the details page.
5.  Add to `.env.local`: `EMAIL_VERIFY_APILAYER_KEY=your_key_here`
